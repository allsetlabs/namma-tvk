## State Debt

| Year | Debt-to-GSDP Ratio | Total Debt |
|------|-------------------|------------|
| 2019-20 | 24.35% | — |
| 2021-22 | **28.83%** (peak) | — |
| 2022-23 | 28.39% | — |
| 2023-24 | 28.00% | — |
| 2024-25 | **26.41%** | **₹8,33,361.80 crore** |
| 2025-26 (est.) | 25.75% | — |

- Under DMK rule (2021–present), debt-to-GSDP **jumped from 24.35% to 28.83%** in first two years — a 4.5 percentage point surge
- Fiscal Deficit at 3.32% in 2023-24, **above the 3% FRBM target**
- Sources:

## Per Capita Income

- Real GSDP per capita grew **35% during 2014–21** (pre-DMK growth)
- Tamil Nadu has **2nd largest state economy** in India
- Contributes ~8% to India's GDP
- Slowing growth post-2021 due to fiscal pressure, high debt servicing

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economy_of_Tamil_Nadu

## Budget Overview 2024-25

- Total Expenditure: ₹4,12,504 crore (12% increase over 2023-24 RE)
- Healthcare: **5% of expenditure** (national avg 6.2%) — BELOW average
- Education: **13.7% of expenditure** (below TVK's 18% target)
- Revenue deficit remains a concern — state borrowing to fund current expenditure
