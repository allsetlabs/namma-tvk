## Farmer Suicides (NCRB Data)

| Year | Tamil Nadu Suicides | National Rank | National Total |
|------|--------------------|-----------|----|
| 2022 | **728** | 4th highest | ~11,000+ |
| 2023 | 67 | Lower | 10,786 |

**Important caveat:** 2023 drop from 728→67 is highly contested:
- Tamil farmer advocacy organizations dispute NCRB methodology
- NCRB classification changes reduced "farmer" category by excluding certain farm labour categories
- Real agrarian distress unchanged — see NFSA implementation gaps, MSP delays

## National Context

- Nationally: **one farmer/farm labourer dies by suicide every hour** (NCRB 2023)
- 112,000+ suicides in India's farming sector over 10 years (Statista)
- Maharashtra (38%) and Karnataka (22%) lead; Tamil Nadu significant contributor

## Welfare Scheme Issues

- **PM-Kisan**: ₹6,000/year to small/marginal farmers — widely cited as inadequate
- **MSP delays**: Tamil Nadu's state procurement often delayed, farmers forced to sell below MSP to private traders
- State welfare scheme disbursement: anecdotal evidence of delays at district level (requires RTI for exact data)

## For Website Use

- **Website stat "14,000+ farmer suicides"** (cited in StatsSection.tsx) — this appears to reference cumulative Tamil Nadu figure over multiple years; 2022 NCRB alone = 728, over 10+ years = thousands
- Recommend citing: "728 farmer suicides in Tamil Nadu in 2022 alone (NCRB)" for accuracy
- Before/After framing: Farmer MSP promise vs delivery gap is the stronger narrative than suicide numbers alone
- **TVK Promise**: Guaranteed MSP, crop insurance within 48 hours
