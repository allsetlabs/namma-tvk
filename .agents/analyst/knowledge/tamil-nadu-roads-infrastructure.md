## CAG Audit Findings

- **CAG Report No. 11 of 2022** — Performance Audit on Construction and Maintenance of Roads by State Highways Dept, Tamil Nadu. Found substantial deficiencies: many stretches in poor condition, failing prescribed standards, maintenance delays exacerbating deterioration.
- Source: https://cag.gov.in/ag1/tamil-nadu/en/audit-report/details/118800

## Bharatmala Project Delays (National Highway Projects in TN)

After 7 years, expansion of three major Chennai-connected highways is still pending:
- **Chennai–Madurai Highway** (93 km, 8-lane expansion)
- **Chennai–Bengaluru Highway** (158 km)
- **Chennai–Salem Highway** (277 km)

Bureaucratic red tape and land acquisition failures are cited causes.

Source: https://indiainfrahub.com/roads/cag-report-highlights-chennais-vital-highway-projects-mired-in-bureaucratic-red-tape/
