## Major Scams Under DMK Government (2021–2026)

### 1. TASMAC Liquor Scam
- **Estimated size:** ₹1,000 crore
- ED raided TASMAC headquarters, depots, and distilleries on **March 6, 2025**
- Alleged: manipulated tenders, overpriced bottles, kickbacks
- Based on **46 DVAC FIRs** from 2017-2023
- **Senthil Balaji** (DMK minister, now Energy Minister): arrested June 2023 by ED in cash-for-jobs scam during his tenure as Transport Minister; later granted bail
- Sources: 
  - https://thefederal.com/category/states/south/tamil-nadu/tasmac-revenue-ed-raid-corruption-charge-senthil-balaji-183075
  - https://swarajyamag.com/tamil-nadu/liquor-lies-and-looming-elections-tamil-nadus-1000-crore-tasmac-scandal

### 2. Illegal Sand Mining Scam
- **Estimated size:** ₹4,000–4,700 crore (ED investigation)
- Allegations reach ₹60,000 crore (expelled DMK cadre allegations, unverified)
- **September 12, 2023:** ED raided 34 locations across 6 districts, targeted 8 sand mining yards
- **February 2025:** Madras High Court ordered CBI investigation into beach sand mining (Thoothukudi, Tirunelveli, Kanniyakumari)
- **ED seized assets worth ₹130 crore** (Feb 2024) — close allies of DMK in dock
- **K. Ponmudy** (DMK minister): accused of laundering proceeds from excess red sand mining
- Court cited: "corruption, collusion, and conspiratorial connivance between politicians, government officials, and private mining firms"
- Sources:
  - https://swarajyamag.com/tamil-nadu/illegal-sand-mining-in-tamil-nadu-the-4000-crore-battle-between-ed-and-dmk
  - https://organiser.org/2024/02/02/219773/bharat/tamil-nadu-ed-seizes-assets-worth-nearly-rs-130-crore-in-sand-mining-case-close-allies-of-dmk-in-dock/

### 3. Historical Corruption (AIADMK era — for comparison)
- Beach sand mining irregularities traced back to 2002-03 to 2016 under previous governments
- Madras HC amicus curiae report: State must recover ₹5,832 crore from miners holding 64 leases

## Bribery Perception

- **62% of Tamil Nadu citizens** reported paying a bribe in the last 1 year (LocalCircles 2019 survey — pre-DMK baseline)
- Breakdown: 41% to property/land registration, 19% to Municipal Corporation, 15% to police
- Source: https://www.localcircles.com/a/press/page/tamil-nadu-corruption-survey-2019
